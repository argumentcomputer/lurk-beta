Expand description

Creates a LEM Func corresponding to Lurk’s step function from a Lang. The ivc flag tells whether the generated step function is used for IVC or NIVC. In the IVC case, the step function is capable of reducing calls to the coprocessors present in Lang and their circuit will go in the circuit of the step function. In the NIVC case, the step function won’t be able to reduce calls to coprocessors and sets up a loop via the Expr::Cproc tag, meaning that the reduction must be done from outside.
